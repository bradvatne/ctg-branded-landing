VIP table booking that pre-sells the deck

The highest-margin inventory in the venue is the part still being sold by conversation at most clubs. Clubtech turns it into a transaction.

  • Map-first, not grid-first. Guests explore your floor from a 3D birds-eye view, tap the exact booth or table, watch 360° walkthroughs of the room, and commit before the price reveals. They buy the spot, not "a table."
  • Tiered packages with stackable add-ons. Bed-only through party package to the ultimate tier, plus bottles, cakes, and transfers added in a tap — the upsell happens at booking, not at the rope.
  • Prepayment kills the no-show table. Deposits, minimum spends, and prepayment mean the group arrives or the revenue stays with you. Dynamic pricing by demand and daypart, plus a "Book Online & Save" spread, pulls commitment forward — so you read Saturday's floor days ahead.

This is the same mechanic that took FINNS Beach Club from on-the-day bookings with no financial guarantee to, in CEO Beau Whittington's words, "millions of dollars worth of pre-paid bookings each month." Guaranteed inventory behaves differently from promised inventory.

The floor and the door, before doors open

Nightclub management software has to run the service day, not just take the reservation. Clubtech runs both sides of the rope.

  • Interactive floor plan with automatic allocation. Online sales sync to an internal floor map in real time, and automatic seating allocation places parties as they book — inventory never double-sells.
  • Door and guest-list VMS. Guest-list and visitor management replaces the clipboard and the promoters' phones, and native events booking with tiered tickets and QR-code check-in runs the ticketed nights.
  • Operator console. By 9pm it shows where tonight's revenue is sitting: which booths are prepaid, what each table ordered, who's on the list. Hosts greet confirmed parties by name instead of triaging a queue of maybes.

When the floor sells out, priority and free-entry capture takes the guest's details anyway — sold out is not a lost guest. See the operations overview for how the service day runs.

Questions operators ask

What is nightclub management software?

Nightclub management software runs the operational side of a club — table bookings, the floor plan, the door and guest list, ticketing, payments, and marketing — from one system. Clubtech combines all of that into a single platform, white-label on the venue's domain, so reservations, front-of-house operations, and marketing share one guest record instead of living in four disconnected tools.

What should a club management system include?

At minimum: a way to sell tables, an operator floor plan that reflects those sales in real time, door and guest-list management, and reporting you can act on. Clubtech adds map-first VIP table booking, automatic seating allocation, an operator console, a real-time ads loop, and 20+ built-in reports — so the system runs the whole night, not just the reservation.

Is there a nightclub management app that works on mobile?

Yes. Clubtech is mobile-first because 82% of bookings happen on a phone after 10pm. Guests book in four taps with sub-second load, no app install, and Apple Pay or Google Pay, and operators run the floor and door from the same platform. The venue's guests never download anything — the club management app runs in the browser on the venue's own domain.

How does club management software reduce no-shows?

By taking payment at booking. Prepayments, deposits, and minimum spends turn a promised table into a guaranteed one — the group arrives or the revenue stays with you. Because prepayment happens days ahead, gaps surface early enough to resell the booth instead of discovering the hole at midnight.

Does nightclub software replace our POS?

No. Clubtech sits on top of your existing POS and PMS with no major IT changes, so switching is low-cost. It handles booking, the floor plan, the door, and marketing; your POS keeps handling in-venue transactions. The two run side by side rather than one replacing the other.
