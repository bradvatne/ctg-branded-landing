Grow

Turn bookings into a marketing engine.

Every booking on the platform is a signal — of revenue, of intent, and of who to reach next. Clubtech sends that signal back to the ad platforms, captures the whole party for the venue, and turns booking patterns into guest intelligence across 20+ reports.

Abandoned-booking recovery

Bring the guest back to the booking they left.

Clubtech sends an abandoned-cart CAPI event within seconds, with the zone, date, and price the guest was considering. That gives the venue’s campaigns the booking context needed to return the guest to the exact place they left.

  • Abandoned-cart CAPI events fire within seconds of a guest leaving checkout
  • The event carries the zone, date, and price needed for a relevant return journey
  • Campaign setup and media buying stay in the venue’s ad accounts; Clubtech supplies the booking signal

Ads & attribution

The booking is the conversion.

When the conversion event is a paid booking with a real value, the ad platforms stop guessing. Clubtech closes the loop between the campaign and the revenue it produced.

  • Real-time conversions to Meta, Google Ads, and GA4 with revenue posted back for value-based optimization
  • Abandoned-cart CAPI events within seconds of a guest leaving checkout
  • Lookalike audiences seeded from your highest-lifetime-value bookers, not follower counts

Every-guest data

Own the audience, not only the booking.

A reservation is who booked, when, from where, for how much, and how far ahead. Clubtech turns that into an owned asset — not a report locked inside a marketplace that rents you your own guests.

  • The booker invites the group, and each invited guest supplies their own details
  • A reservation for eight grows the venue’s marketable audience by eight, not one
  • Every guest record belongs to the venue, under its own white-label booking journey
  • Guest-list, ticket, and reservation data meet in the same owned audience

Guest intelligence

Read the business across 20+ reports.

Booking patterns become an owned operating asset: revenue by hour, lead time, product mix, origin markets, and repeat share, segmented and trending inside the platform.

  • Revenue by hour and daypart shows which shifts actually pay
  • Lead time and average value by variant show how far ahead each product sells and what it earns
  • Origin markets and repeat-customer share show who returns and where demand comes from
  • 20+ reports are included across the guest, the operation, and revenue

FAQ

Questions operators ask.

Does Clubtech manage my ad campaigns automatically?

+

No. Clubtech sends paid-booking values and abandoned-cart events to Meta, Google, and GA4 so those platforms can optimize and retarget against real booking intent. Campaign setup and media buying remain in the venue’s ad accounts.

What reports does Clubtech include?

+

More than 20, spanning the guest, the operation, and revenue — including revenue by hour, lead time by daypart, average value by package, repeat-customer share, and origin markets. They are read inside the platform, not exported to a spreadsheet.

Who owns the guest data?

+

The venue. Clubtech is white-label, so every guest record belongs to the venue. Because the booker invites their group, the whole party enters the database, not just the person who paid.
