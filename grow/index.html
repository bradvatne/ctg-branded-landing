Grow

Turn bookings into a marketing engine.

Every booking on the platform is a signal — of revenue, of intent, and of who to reach next. Clubtech turns that signal into value-based ad optimization, automated retargeting, an owned guest database, and feedback from the guests who actually came.

Marketing AI

Optimize on revenue, not clicks.

Most venues run ads against a proxy — a click, a page view, a form. Clubtech runs them against the real thing. Every booking becomes a value-based conversion, and abandoned bookings retarget themselves — the "drive revenue while you sleep" mechanic, running whether or not anyone is watching the dashboard.

  • Every booking fires to Meta, Google, and GA4 with its actual revenue value attached
  • Abandoned-booking retargeting within seconds — dynamic ads return the guest to the exact zone, date, and price
  • Campaigns learn from money in the bank, so spend flows toward the guests who spend

Ads & attribution

The booking is the conversion.

When the conversion event is a paid booking with a real value, the ad platforms stop guessing. Clubtech closes the loop between the campaign and the revenue it produced.

  • Real-time conversions to Meta, Google Ads, and GA4 with revenue posted back for value-based optimization
  • Abandoned-cart CAPI events within seconds of a guest leaving checkout
  • Lookalike audiences seeded from your highest-lifetime-value bookers, not follower counts

Guest data & reports

The data your bookings already make.

A reservation is who booked, when, from where, for how much, and how far ahead. Clubtech turns that into an owned asset — not a report locked inside a marketplace that rents you your own guests.

  • 20+ reports across the guest, the operation, and revenue — read inside the platform, no CSV export
  • Revenue by hour, lead time by daypart, average value by package, repeat-customer share, origin markets
  • The whole party enters the database, and every record belongs to the venue

Clubtech Reviews

Hear from the guests who actually came.

The platform knows exactly who visited, when, and what they bought — so feedback requests go to verified guests, not anonymous internet traffic. Every response ties back to a real booking.

  • Feedback from real bookers, traceable to the zone, package, and daypart behind it
  • A private channel before a public one — catch the problem before it reaches Google or a travel site
  • Feedback sits alongside spend history, sharpening the segments the platform already builds

FAQ

Questions operators ask.

Does Clubtech manage my ad campaigns automatically?

+

Clubtech automates the parts that move revenue: it posts every booking to Meta, Google, and GA4 as a value-based conversion and retargets abandoned bookings on its own within seconds. It feeds your ad platforms real revenue data so they optimize toward paying guests — it is not a replacement for your ad accounts or media buyer.

What reports does Clubtech include?

+

More than 20, spanning the guest, the operation, and revenue — including revenue by hour, lead time by daypart, average value by package, repeat-customer share, and origin markets. They are read inside the platform, not exported to a spreadsheet.

Who owns the guest data?

+

The venue. Clubtech is white-label, so every guest record belongs to the venue. Because the booker invites their group, the whole party enters the database, not just the person who paid.
