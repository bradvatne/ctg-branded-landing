FAQ

Before you book.

What happens on the demo?

+

Fifteen focused minutes. We show the booking flow, the operations console, and the marketing loop on your kind of inventory, then map the scope behind a commercial proposal. It stays specific to your venue — no slide-ware.

Do I need to prepare anything?

+

No. It helps to bring the people who own the number — owner or GM, revenue, marketing, and front-of-house — but nothing needs to be prepared in advance. The optional notes box just lets us tailor the walkthrough to what matters most to you.

What does Clubtech cost?

+

Pricing is built around your confirmed inventory, systems, and rollout, so we cover it live on the call and follow up with a proposal tailored to your operation. There are no contracts and no credit card needed to see the platform.

Do we have to replace our POS or PMS?

+

No. Clubtech integrates with your existing POS, PMS, payments, and ad platforms rather than replacing them, so it sits on top of the systems your team already runs.

Is Clubtech white-label, and who owns the guest data?

+

Yes — your domain, your design, and your guest data. There is no marketplace between you and your guest. Because the booker invites their group, the whole party enters your database, not just the person who paid.

How soon could we go live?

+

We deliver in five stages, configured around the venue you run. We’ll walk through the timeline for your setup on the call so you know exactly what going live involves.
