A resort sells more than rooms. The pool deck, the cabanas, the day passes, the beach club, the events — each is a revenue line that too often runs on a clipboard, a phone, and whoever emailed the manager first. The room is booked months out; the daybed is still a walk-up hope. That gap is money.

Clubtech closes it across the property. Guests choose the exact cabana, daybed, or day pass on a 3D birds-eye map, pick a package, and prepay — before they arrive, on the resort's own domain. Reservations, front-of-house operations, and marketing run in one platform, connected to the systems the front office already trusts. Clubtech processes $332k in weekly GMV across venue partners in more than 7 countries.

## Every outlet on one booking surface

- **Pools, cabanas, and day passes sell like the premium product they are.** Guests explore zones on the map, see what each package includes, and commit before price is the point — so the best furniture sells out first, not last.
- **Hotel guests and outside demand, one inventory.** Day passes open the property to non-staying guests without a second system, and the floor sees every booking on the same live map.
- **Dynamic pricing per daypart.** A sunbed on a peak Saturday and a quiet Tuesday are priced to demand, so the same furniture earns what it is worth.

## Connected to the front office, owned by the resort

- **Opera PMS integration** meets the front office where it lives, so the booking layer sits on top of the stack rather than replacing it.
- **White-label** — the booking runs on the resort's domain and design, and every guest record belongs to the resort, not a marketplace.
- **Every guest captured.** The booker invites the party, and each guest supplies their own details, so a cabana for eight grows the marketable database by eight.

## Questions operators ask

### Can Clubtech handle a resort with multiple outlets?

Yes. Pools, cabanas, day passes, a beach club, and events run on one platform and one live inventory, so each outlet is bookable and visible to the floor without a separate system per venue.

### Does it work with our hotel systems?

Clubtech integrates with Opera PMS and sits on top of the existing POS and PMS stack with no major IT changes. It takes over the booking journey, the live floor plan, and the marketing loop while the front office keeps running.

### Can we sell day passes to guests who aren't staying with us?

Yes. Day passes let a resort open the pool deck and cabanas to outside demand through the same booking flow, with prepayment collected before arrival and the guest captured in the resort's own database.
