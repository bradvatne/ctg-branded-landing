A resort sells more than rooms. Pools, cabanas, day passes, beach inventory, and events can each become pre-paid products without forcing the property into a marketplace or a replacement project.

Clubtech connects those sellable outlets through one booking, operations, and marketing platform. The resort keeps the brand and guest data; the exact property integration boundary is agreed during technical review.

## Workflow — From outlet inventory to one operating view

1. **Define the outlet and inventory.** Map the pool, beach, cabanas, daybeds, zones, packages, or event tickets the property wants to sell.
2. **Open the direct booking journey.** Guests choose exact inventory, add a package, and prepay under the resort's brand.
3. **Connect the service day.** Online sales update the internal floor plan and operator console.
4. **Read property demand.** Booking volume, lead time, product mix, and repeat behavior feed guest intelligence and 20+ reports.

## Capabilities — One platform, distinct outlet rules

- **Pools and cabanas:** exact-furniture booking, packages, prepayment, and dynamic pricing.
- **Day passes:** a direct, white-label channel for inventory the property chooses to release.
- **Events:** tiered tickets and QR-code check-in alongside reservations.
- **Guest data:** every-guest capture, attribution, and an owned audience across bookings.

## Questions operators ask

### Can Clubtech handle more than one resort outlet?

Clubtech supports pools, cabanas, day passes, beach inventory, events, and other approved furniture- or zone-led booking flows. The exact outlet scope is configured with the property.

### Does Clubtech replace the PMS or POS?

No. It sits on top of the existing stack. Integration behavior is confirmed during technical review.

### Can the resort sell directly rather than through a marketplace?

Yes. Clubtech is white-label, so the booking runs on the resort's domain and design, with the guest data owned by the property.
