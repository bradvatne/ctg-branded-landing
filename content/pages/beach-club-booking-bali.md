Bali is where Clubtech has its clearest public beach-club proof. FINNS Beach Club runs its booking journey on Clubtech, moving from on-the-day bookings with no financial guarantee to millions of dollars in pre-paid bookings each month, in CEO Beau Whittington's account.

This page applies that proven operating model to the inventory a Bali beach club actually sells: a specific daybed, cabana, table, zone, package, and daypart.

## Workflow — From exact daybed to pre-paid booking

1. **Put the venue on the map.** Guests explore a 3D birds-eye view and choose the exact furniture or zone they want.
2. **Attach the package.** Tiered packages and approved add-ons sit inside the booking flow rather than waiting for an on-deck upsell.
3. **Commit before arrival.** Prepayments, deposits, and minimum spends turn a held spot into committed revenue.
4. **Send the sale to the floor.** Online inventory syncs to the internal floor plan so the operator sees what is booked before doors open.
