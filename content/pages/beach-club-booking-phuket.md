A beach club does not need one flat price for every date, daypart, and position. It needs a booking system that can distinguish the front row from the shade line, attach the right package, and show the operator what has committed before doors open.

For Phuket operators, this page focuses on that pricing-and-demand job. It does not rely on unsupported local season, payment, or guest-behavior statistics.

## Workflow — Make the deck bookable by zone and demand

1. **Map the furniture and zones.** Guests choose a specific daybed, cabana, table, or area from the 3D birds-eye view.
2. **Build the package ladder.** Bed-only and higher-value packages can carry approved add-ons inside the flow.
3. **Set pricing by demand.** Rates can vary by date, daypart, and inventory rather than remaining flat.
4. **Take prepayment.** Deposits, prepayments, and minimum spends commit revenue before arrival.
