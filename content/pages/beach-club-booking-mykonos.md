When the selling calendar is compressed, slow feedback is expensive. The venue needs to know which positions are selling, how far ahead guests commit, and which package ladder is working while there is still time to adjust.

For Mykonos operators, this page focuses on that fast-feedback job. It does not claim local customer results, typical lead times, or unverified market behavior.

## Workflow — Sell the position, then read the pattern

1. **Show the exact inventory.** Guests select a specific bed, cabana, table, or zone on the 3D birds-eye map.
2. **Attach value before arrival.** Packages, approved add-ons, minimum spends, and prepayment sit inside checkout.
3. **Price to demand.** Dynamic pricing can distinguish dates, dayparts, and premium positions.
4. **Follow the booking into the floor.** Sold inventory updates the internal floor plan and operator view.
5. **Read the result.** Guest intelligence and 20+ reports show booking volume, lead time, product mix, and repeat behavior.
