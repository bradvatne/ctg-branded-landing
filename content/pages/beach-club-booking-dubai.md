Dubai venue evaluations often bring commercial, operations, brand, and hotel IT concerns into the same room. Clubtech gives that group one product to evaluate: exact-inventory booking under the venue's brand, connected to floor operations and revenue reporting.

The operating case is not a claim about local season length. It is simpler: fixed premium furniture should be priced and sold deliberately before the service day.

## Workflow — Price the zone, sell the package, run the floor

1. **Model the exact inventory.** Daybeds, cabanas, tables, and zones appear on the 3D birds-eye booking map.
2. **Set packages and pricing.** Tiered packages, approved add-ons, minimum spends, and demand-based pricing sit against the inventory.
3. **Take commitment online.** Guests choose and pay through a mobile-first, multi-currency booking journey under the venue's brand.
4. **Carry the booking into operations.** Sold inventory updates the internal floor plan and operator view.
