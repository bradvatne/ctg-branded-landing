## What cookies are

        Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They let a site remember your actions and preferences over time. We use cookies and similar technologies to keep this site working, to understand how it is used, and — only with your consent — to measure the performance of our advertising.

        Nothing in our **Analytics** or **Marketing** categories loads until you click **Accept** in the cookie banner. You can change your choices at any time using the button below.

          [Manage cookie preferences](#)

## Categories we use

### Strictly necessary

        Remembers your consent choice and keeps the site secure. **Always on.** Lawful basis: legitimate interest (the ePrivacy §5(3) exemption for strictly necessary cookies).

### Analytics

        Helps us understand which pages are useful and where visitors drop off. **Off by default; opt-in.** Lawful basis: consent.

### Marketing

        Measures paid-campaign performance and lets us show you relevant ads on other platforms. **Off by default; opt-in.** Lawful basis: consent.

## Managing your choices

        If you reject non-essential cookies, the consent layer immediately deletes any of the cookies listed above that have already been set under our domain; reloading the page clears the tag scripts loaded during this session.

        If your browser sends the Global Privacy Control (Sec-GPC) signal, we treat it as a verified opt-out and automatically persist a rejected consent state — you do not need to interact with the banner.
