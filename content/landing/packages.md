## The upsell that never takes a night off

The best time to sell a guest more is while they are already buying. Clubtech builds the upsell into the booking flow, so packages and add-ons are offered at the moment of intent — not by a staff member who has to remember to ask, and not after the guest has arrived and moved on.

## How it works

**Tiered packages.** Guests choose a level — bed only, party package, the full experience — and see exactly what each includes. The tiers do the selling: most guests move up when the next level is one tap away.

**Stackable add-ons.** Bottles, cakes, transfers, and products stack onto any booking. Each one is incremental revenue on top of the reservation, collected before arrival.

**Priced your way.** Packages and add-ons are configured to how the venue actually sells, not forced into a generic template.

## Why it earns

Add-ons are margin the venue was already capable of selling and often left on the table. Because they run inside the flow — 24 hours a day, on the phone, at the moment of commitment — they lift average order value without adding a single conversation on the floor.

## Questions operators ask

### What add-ons can guests buy during booking?

Bottles, cakes, transfers, and other products a venue configures. Add-ons stack onto any booking and are paid for inside the flow, before the guest arrives.

### Do packages work for both bookings and events?

Yes. Tiered packages and add-ons run on the same platform as reservations and ticketed events, so the upsell mechanics are consistent across everything a venue sells.

### Does upselling require more staff?

No. The upsell runs inside the booking flow around the clock, so guests build a larger order themselves — the venue captures the incremental spend without adding work on the floor.
