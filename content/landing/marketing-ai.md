## Optimize on revenue, not clicks

Most venues run ads against a proxy — a click, a page view, a form. Clubtech runs them against the real thing. Every booking fires to Meta, Google Ads, and GA4 in real time, with the actual revenue value posted back. Your campaigns learn from money in the bank, so the platform spends toward the guests who spend, not the ones who scroll.

## Retargeting that runs itself

A guest who abandons checkout is the highest-intent audience you have. Clubtech sends an abandoned-booking event within seconds, and dynamic ads return that guest to the exact zone, date, and price they were looking at. It is the "drive revenue while you sleep" mechanic — automated, and running whether or not anyone is watching the dashboard.

## Audiences built from real spend

Lookalikes are seeded from your highest-lifetime-value bookers — the guests who actually paid the most — not from follower counts or vanity lists. And because the booker invites their whole party, every reservation grows the marketable audience by the size of the group, not by one.

## Where this is heading

Clubtech's attribution and retargeting loop is live today across Meta and Google. Deeper per-venue ad automation is on the roadmap; we build toward it in the open and will say plainly what is shipped when it ships — never before.

## Questions operators ask

### Does Clubtech manage my ad campaigns automatically?

Clubtech automates the parts that move revenue: it posts every booking to Meta, Google, and GA4 as a value-based conversion, and it retargets abandoned bookings on its own within seconds. It feeds your ad platforms real revenue data so they optimize toward paying guests. It is not a replacement for your ad accounts or your media buyer — it makes both far more accurate.

### What does "value-based optimization" mean?

Instead of telling the ad platforms a booking happened, Clubtech tells them how much revenue it was worth. The platforms then bid toward the guests likely to spend the most, rather than treating a $200 booking and a $2,000 booking as the same event.

### How does abandoned-booking retargeting work?

When a guest leaves before paying, Clubtech fires an abandoned-booking event within seconds and dynamic ads bring them back to the exact spot, date, and price they were viewing — no manual list-building required.
