## Add a layer, don't rip one out

The fear with new venue software is the migration. Clubtech avoids it. The platform sits on top of the POS and PMS a venue already runs, with no major IT changes. Your service and accounting teams keep the systems they trust; Clubtech takes over the layer those systems were never built for — selling furniture, zones, and dayparts online, running the floor against that inventory, and feeding the ad platforms real revenue.

## What Clubtech connects to

**Property & operations.** Opera PMS, so hotel bookings meet the front office where it lives.

**Payments.** Airwallex for multi-currency card processing, Midtrans for Southeast Asian methods — QRIS, GoPay, and OVO — plus Apple Pay and Google Pay for one-touch wallet checkout. Guests pay the way their market pays, and prepayment clears inside the flow.

**Marketing.** Meta Ads, Google Ads, and GA4 — the conversion loop that posts every booking back as revenue for value-based optimization.

**Messaging.** WhatsApp, the channel guests in Southeast Asia and the Gulf actually answer.

## Built for the markets you sell to

Multi-currency is native, so a guest in Bali pays the way Bali pays and a guest in Dubai settles in their card's currency. The integrations are matched to how each market books and pays, not bolted on as a single generic list.

## Questions operators ask

### Does Clubtech replace our POS?

No. Clubtech sits on top of the existing POS and PMS stack rather than replacing it, with no major IT changes. It takes over the booking journey, the live floor plan, and the marketing loop while your current systems keep running.

### What payment methods are supported?

Multi-currency card processing through Airwallex, Apple Pay and Google Pay for wallet checkout, and Midtrans for Southeast Asian methods — QRIS, GoPay, and OVO. Prepayment is collected inside the booking flow.

### Does Clubtech work with hotel systems?

Yes — Opera PMS is a named integration, which is why the platform fits hotel pools and resort venues where the front office is central.
