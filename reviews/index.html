Platform · Clubtech Reviews

Hear from the guests who actually came.

Feedback tied to real bookings, not anonymous internet traffic. Clubtech Reviews closes the loop the booking opens — so you hear from the guest who visited, before they reach a public site.

Book a Demo See guest data

Feedback from a real visit, not a random review

The booking platform knows exactly who visited, when, and what they bought. Clubtech Reviews uses that to ask the right people for feedback — verified guests who were actually there — instead of chasing anonymous internet traffic. Every response ties back to a real booking, which makes the signal useful: you can see which zone, which package, or which daypart underdelivered.

A private channel before a public one

An unhappy guest should have somewhere to be heard before they reach Google or a travel site. Clubtech Reviews gives them that channel — a straightforward, honest mechanic operators understand: catch the problem while you can still fix the relationship.

It sharpens what the platform already knows

Feedback sits alongside spend history, so the segments the platform already builds get sharper. A guest's opinion is more useful next to what they actually booked and paid.

Questions operators ask

How is Clubtech Reviews different from a public review site?

Every response is tied to an actual booking made through the platform, so feedback comes from verified guests who visited — not anonymous traffic. That makes it useful for operations, because you can trace a comment to the zone, package, and daypart behind it.

Where does the feedback go?

Into a private channel the venue sees, and alongside the guest's spend history in the platform's data — so it informs both service recovery and the segments used for marketing.

Is it live?

Yes, Clubtech Reviews is in production and captures post-visit feedback from guests who booked through the platform.
